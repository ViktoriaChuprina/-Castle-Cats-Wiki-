The facts

Benedict is almost certainly a reference to the character Sherlock Holmes from Sir Arthur Conan Doyle's book series and to the actor Benedict Cumberbatch, who plays Holmes in a television adaptation called Sherlock.

  • "You see, but you do not observe," is a direct quote from A Scandal in Bohemia, published in 1892.
  • The beginning of Benedict's Hero Book description, "Elementary, dear Guild Leader...", is derived from the line, "Elementary, my dear Watson," a quote often attributed to Sherlock Holmes but never actually said by him in any of the books.
  • "I spy with my little eye..." is a reference to the children's game I Spy, which dates back to the 19th century.
  • Benedict is a participant in Ember, Catania's version of the dating app Tinder that has appeared in the game during Valentine Events since 2021; his profile reads as follows: The young ones made me do this… I have been alone most of my life – by choice. I cannot afford distractions. I may be old, but I am still a working cat.
