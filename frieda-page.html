The facts

Mewton's name, skills, and description appear to be references to Sir Isaac Newton , an influential scientist who formulated his theory of gravity after observing an apple fall from a tree, although the part about it hitting him on the head is not reliable information.

  • Frieda was originally called Kattja, who was created as an original character, long before Castle Cats was developed, by Anna "Kamirah" Larsson, the former Art Director at PocApp Studios.
  • Anna has said at her DeviantArt profile: "After the release of Castle Cats I've gotten a lot of questions regarding Kattja since I decided to make her a hero in the game. Kattja belongs to me and I designed her 15 years ago. So she's my personal character that's just helping the Castle Cats out defeating the Evil Pugomancer."
  • Kattja's name and appearance in-game were changed so that Kamirah would be free to do whatever she wished with Kattja.
  • Frieda put her artistic skills to use designing a garden for the guild's gardening competition in the Spring Festival 2020 Quest.
