The facts

Capri was the Facebook winner of the June 2017 Design a Castle Cat contest[1], designed by Mandy Gurtatowski.

  • Capri is likely a reference to Shenron, the dragon in the Dragon Ball/Dragon Ball Z franchise
  • Capri's Hero Book entry provides some backstory that parallels Shenron's: he grants a wish when the seven Dragon Balls are gathered.
  • Some of her lines, such as the one asking about finding crystal balls, reinforce this association.
