The facts

Sting is likely named after the sword of the same name in The Hobbit and The Lord of the Rings by J.R.R. Tolkien.

  • Sting's Hero Book entry says his necklace, which is greenish-blue, starts glowing when dogs are close, just as the blade glows blue when orcs are near.
