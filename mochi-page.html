The facts

Dango is named after a Japanese sweet called Dango.

  • Mochi is named after a Japanese food called Mochi.
  • As a Dynamic Duo cat, she is paired with Dango, another hero named for a Japanese food.
  • Mochi's line, "One wrong step and...", might refer to the belief that ninjas were able to walk on water.
