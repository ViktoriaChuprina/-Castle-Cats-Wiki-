The facts

Pipsqueak was modeled after a real-life cat named Pipen belonging to a PocApp Studios developaw.

  • Pipsqueak claims to be a princess but this has never been confirmed; her fur pattern resembles that of the historic King Basil but not of the current Crown Princess Adelaide, with whom she has an odd and otherwise unexplained exchange in the Summer 2022 Event Quest.
  • Although usually pictured with her eyes closed, Pipsqueak’s irises were revealed to be blue in a promotional image and later confirmed in-game during the Valentine 2020 Event Quest, in which Pipsqueak and Molotov begin a romantic relationship.
  • The couple has a kitten named Ash, who was added to the game as an Alchemy cat in the same Valentine Event 2020.
