The facts

Her line, "Call me, beep me, if you want to reach me," is most likely a reference to the Kim Possible theme song, sung by Christina Milian.

  • Angel's Hero Book description is likely a reference to a pickup line, "Did it hurt when you fell from heaven?"
  • Her line, "Who is Mercy?", is probably a reference to the Overwatch character Mercy, whose outfits and abilities are frequently angelically derived.
