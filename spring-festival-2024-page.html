Spring Festival 2024

Spring Festival 2024 was a full Castle Cats event added in app version 4.4.0. It began on March 4, received a mid-event update on March 27, and ran through April 20.

The event introduced six new heroes: Grace, Lee, and Mai who could be purchased with real money, and three more who could be crafted in Alchemy. All 37 of the previous Spring Festival cats were also available in various ways.

New guild leader customization options were available in the Event Shop, as were one new and nine returning hero outfits. Items from past Spring Festival events were available in the prize pool of the Event Box. The Event Badge and Event Pass offered progressive rewards as players meet objectives based on event activities.

Event Materials

While the event was live, rewards for event-specific, regular and battle quests at quest level 4 and above could include eggshells, feathers, and paintbrushes, Spring Festival event materials that could be used to craft hero eggs and plushies and acquire new guild leader items. These materials could also be purchased as microtransactions in the Event Shop.

The quest selection screen included a materials boost button offering the option to watch an ad and get double event materials from quest rewards for 30 minutes. Alternatively, purchasing a new premium hero for real money doubled event materials from quest rewards and purchasing two or more microtransaction cats tripled them. These boosts lasted for the duration of the event and replaced the ad boost option.

Guild Leader Items

The Event Box  is shown in the final tab of the Event Shop window. It can be purchased for 50,000 coins every two days or for 60 gems otherwise at any time. Players get a free box at the start of the event as well and can also earn free box tickets by completing certain stages of the Event Pass. For each box purchased, a player receives one of the nine prizes shown in the window, chosen at random. Note that the animation that plays upon purchase is strictly cosmetic; each of the nine items has an equal chance of being selected.
