The facts

Waffles, as Pancake was formerly known, was a premium Charity Event cat offered for sale only once, during the Charity Event 2017 (below left). Like all premium Charity cats, Waffles was a Celebrity Hero, a rendition and namesake of a real-life celebrity cat, available in the game by official arrangement with the cat's real-life owner. His owner received a special image (below right) to commemorate the occasion and promote both the game and the charity of choice.

  • The change from Waffles to Pancake affected not only how the cat could be recruited going forward, but also affected those that already existed in players' guilds, which were automatically converted to Pancake.
  • Pancake's Hero Book description is the same as Waffles' used to be, except the latter also included a link to his real-life counterpart's Instagram page.
  • Waffles' lines were the same as those now said by Pancake, except for the name difference.
  • The cat also underwent a significant redesign as part of the changeover; his former appearance as Waffles is pictured below.
  • The real-life Waffles was a Scottish Fold and his Castle Cats namesake was confirmed to be one as well, but Pancake does not appear to be of that breed.
