The facts

Blink is confirmed female and a reference to the character Tracer from the game Overwatch.

  • Blink and Tracer bear a striking resemblance to each other
  • They both wear light blue goggles and a brown bomber jacket.
  • Blink's orange fur is a close match to the shade of Tracer's skin suit.
  • They both have spiky hair.
  • Blink is the name of one of Tracer's abilities.
  • Blink's Attack Skill, throwing a glowing orb that explodes when it reaches the enemy, mimics Tracer's Pulse Bomb ultimate.
  • Blink's lines about Catania needing more heroes and about deja vu seem to be adapted from two of Tracer's lines: "The world could always use more heroes," and, "Ever get that feeling of déjà vu?"
  • Blink’s line, “My ultimate is charging,” echoes an identical Overwatch line that can be said by players to warn others of an incoming attack.
  • Iko.N is another Castle Cats hero who seems to have an association with a particular Overwatch character.
  • Blink's description says she is "faster than any blue hedgehog," a reference to Sonic the Hedgehog; however, Blink runs much more slowly than Dash, the game's Sonic-inspired character.
  • Blink is one of four Castle Cats heroes who uses a prosthetic in place of a missing leg; the others are Mewton, Mequa, and Wetbeard.
