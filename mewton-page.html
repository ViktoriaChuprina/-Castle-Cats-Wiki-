The facts

Mewton's name, skills, and description appear to be references to Sir Isaac Newton , an influential scientist who formulated his theory of gravity after observing an apple fall from a tree, although the part about it hitting him on the head is not reliable information.

  • History remembers neither Isaac Newton's robotic arm nor his bionic eye patch.
  • Mewton's phrase: "What is the Universe? What is life?" may reflect Newton's quest to understand the universe and his attempts to discover the philosopher's stone.
  • His tousled hair and the phrase "Crazy?" could have been borrowed from the "mad scientist" archetype.
  • Mewton's cyborg parts may be a reference to the video game Warcraft , specifically to one of the Dwarven races.
  • Mewton's supervillain alter ego, Doctor Catstrophe, was likely inspired by Doctor Octopus from the Marvel Universe.
  • Both are scientific geniuses turned villains.
  • Mewton in his villainous costume has, like Doctor Octopus, four mechanical, tentacle-like appendages.
  • Dr. Catstrophe's tendency to end the names of his inventions with "-inator" may be a reference to Dr. Doofenshmirtz from the animated series Phineas and Ferb .
