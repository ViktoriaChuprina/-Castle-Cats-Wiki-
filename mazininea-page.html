The facts

Mawizinea was designed by Natty, a long-time Castle Cats player.

  • Unlike other fan cat submissions, Mawizinea was chosen by the developaws because of the artist's inspiring story and in support of the artist's fight against cancer.
  • Mawizinea is a British Shorthair, a breed characterized by a stocky body, dense coat and broad face.
