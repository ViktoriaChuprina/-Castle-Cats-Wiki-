The facts

Romeo might be an American Shorthair.

  • Romeo is partly based on the male lead in the Shakespearean play Romeo and Juliet
  • One of his lines mentions a balcony, a reference to perhaps the play's most famous scene, wherein Romeo and Juliet first declare their love for each other.
  • His Hero Book description states that he dreams of being an actor, which could also connect to the play.
  • As a Dynamic Duo cat, Romeo is paired with Juliet, Castle Cats' counterpart to the female lead in the play.
  • Romeo and Juliet are usually together when they appear in event stories and were the main characters in the Valentine 2018 Quest.
  • The appearance of Charmer in the same quest caused some jealousy between the couple and hinted at their bisexual nature, which was later confirmed by the developaws.
  • While not explicitly acknowledged in-game, it is widely believed that Julio is the couple's son, as his name is a blending of theirs.
  • Romeo is a participant in Ember, Catania's version of the dating app Tinder that has appeared in the game during Valentine Events since 2021; his profile reads as follows: I came here to compete with my love, but I now[sic] for sure that she shall win, for she is the sun and moon both – beautiful both day and night.
