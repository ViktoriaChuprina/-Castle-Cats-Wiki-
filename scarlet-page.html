The facts

Scarlet is a reference to the Pokémon universe, as "Blobmon" is the word used in the game to refer to Pokémon.

  • In one of his lines, Scarlet says he's a trainer, most likely based on the Pokémon character Red, as scarlet is a synonym for red.
  • Scarlet's lines are inside jokes regarding the franchise.
