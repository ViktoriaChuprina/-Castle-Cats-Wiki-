The facts

Conan can be "promoted" by crafting a special outfit offered on a rotating basis as one of the Monthly Challenges. The outfit is strictly cosmetic and has no special perks or hidden features.

  • Conan, Catniss and Francis are the main heroes of the game, the first to join the guild (after the tutorial hostess), the first three cats in the Hero Book, and the lead characters in the Main Story Quest.
  • While the player's avatar is the guild leader, Conan often acts in a leadership role for the guild during the stories; the existence of a separate guild leader is first suggested in the Call of Heroes 2018 Quest and Conan later explicitly acknowledges that he is not the guild leader in the Summer 2020 Quest.
  • As Conan's description implies he wrote it himself, it has been suggested that he is the author of all of the Hero Book descriptions, and many of them are easily interpreted as being written from his perspective, such as those for Rich, Jin Shu, Fafnir, Ace, Shaun, Star and Atchoum.
  • Conan's locked silhouette is actually visible when purchasing him through the Hero Book for 30 gems.
  • Conan is a confirmed reference to the fictional character Conan the Barbarian.
  • Conan has chiroptophobia, the fear of bats.
  • He exhibits this fear while encountering bats several times during the Halloween 2017 Quest and is teased for it by Francis and Kyle.
  • Conan and Catniss have a friendly relationship that one or the other sometimes appears to wish might be more romantic.
