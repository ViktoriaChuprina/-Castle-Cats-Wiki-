Science Fair 2024

Science Fair 2024 is a full Castle Cats event added in app version 3.9.0. It began on April 22, received a mid-event update on May 15, and is scheduled to run through June 8.

This new event replaces Call of Heroes Events, which had followed Spring Festival Events since 2018. Science Fair Event 2024 incorporates Call of Heroes elements, such as past cats and items, with new Science Fair designs, including new event materials and icons. Past Call of Hero cats may still appear in future Science Fair event stories but with a "new angle".

The event introduces six new heroes: Solo, Tank, and Yoko, who can be purchased with real money, and three more who can be crafted in Alchemy. All 27 of the previous Call of Heroes cats are also available in various ways.

New guild leader customization options are available in the Event Shop, as are one new and nine returning hero outfits. Items from past Call of Heroes events are available in the prize pool of the Event Box. The Event Badge and Event Pass offer progressive rewards as players meet objectives based on event activities.

Event Materials

While the event is live, rewards for event-specific, regular and battle quests at quest level 4 and above can include cat eye bots, stopwatches, and fossils, Science Fair event materials that can be used to craft hero eggs and plushies and acquire new guild leader items. These materials can also be purchased as microtransactions in the Event Shop.

Guild Leader Items

The Event Box  is shown in the final tab of the Event Shop window. It can be purchased for 50,000 coins every two days or for 60 gems otherwise at any time. Players get a free box at the start of the event as well and can also earn free box tickets by completing certain stages of the Event Pass. For each box purchased, a player receives one of the nine prizes shown in the window, chosen at random. Note that the animation that plays upon purchase is strictly cosmetic; each of the nine items has an equal chance of being selected.
