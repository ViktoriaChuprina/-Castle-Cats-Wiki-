The facts

Grubb is most likely based on the orc characters in the Warcraft franchise, going back to the original three real-time strategy games.

  • His greenish color, topknot, and protruding lower teeth make for the spitting image of a typical orc.
  • His dialog is very similar to that of the orcs that make up a basic unit, especially in Warcraft III: Reign of Chaos.
  • Grubb's habit of poking sheep to see if they explode, described in his Hero Book entry, is a reference to an easter egg that first appeared in Warcraft II: Tides of Darkness.
