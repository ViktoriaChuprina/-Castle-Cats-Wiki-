The facts

Mego's Hero Book entry describes him as part of a "brotherhood" of tofu enthusiasts, and he professes a love of tofu in one of his lines, paralleling Yugo's membership in The Brotherhood of the Tofu; interestingly, however, Mego does not have a counterpart to Yugo's companion, Az the Tofu bird.

  • Mego is a confirmed reference to the character Yugo from the television show Wakfu: The Animated Series.
  • One of Mego's lines is, "It's hammer time," and Hammer Time is a type of attack in the Wakfu universe.
  • The line, "I'm a lot older than I look," might be a reference to Yugo's ability to reincarnate.
  • "It's hammer time!" could also be a reference to the MC Hammer song, "U Can’t Touch This", specifically the lyric, "Stop! Hammer time!", which along with variations have become popular catchphrases.
