Daisy
Daisy is a Mythic Valentine Purchase Hero who is not currently available for recruitment.
Daisy is available annually in the Valentine Events, offered originally in 2024 for $4.99 USD and from 2025 onward for 300 gems and as a possible reward in the Event Box.
Daisy will also be eligible to become a Timewalker for 300 gems but has not appeared as one as of yet.

Daisy is a song teacher. In preparation of Valentine's Day, she helps other cats find the courage and skill to sing their emotions.
