The facts

Chief was modeled and named after the real-life cat Chiefen the ragdoll belonging to PocApp Studios developaw Dilla.

  • Chiefen was named after Master Chief from the video game series Halo.
  • Chiefen was also the inspiration for PocApp Studios' first game, Carpet Kitty.
  • Dexter is Chief's little brother.
  • Chief is a Ragdoll[4], a breed characterized by a silky coat with point coloration and blue eyes.
