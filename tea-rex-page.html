The facts

Tea-rex appears to be based on Tyrannosaurus rex, an extinct species of carnivorous dinosaur, as well as dinosaurs in general.

  • The name Tea-Rex is a pun on T. rex as they are both pronounced the same.
  • Some of his features resemble those of dinosaurs, although the plates on his back are associated with Stegasaurs and are not known on Tyrannosaurs.
  • He says himself he is a dinosaur.
