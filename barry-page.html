The facts

Barry is a fairly obvious reference to the wizard student Harry Potter and the eponymous series of novels and films that tell his story

  • Barry was originally named Barry Pawter.
  • An older Hero Book description made direct reference to Harry's forehead scar: "Be grateful for the little things in life, as Barry is. At least his scar doesn't look like a potato."
  • At Rank 4, Barry acquires what appears to be Harry's Patronus, a stag.
  • Barry's line about having "a lot of money... at the bank" might refer to the fact that Harry's parents left him a great sum of money at the Gringotts bank.
