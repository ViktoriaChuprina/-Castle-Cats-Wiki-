The facts

Courage appears to be based on the late Steve Jobs, co-founder of Apple Inc

  • Several quotes by Courage seem to mimic quotes by Steve Jobs:
  • "That's not what we think design is. It's not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works."
  • "Your work is going to fill a large part of your life, and the only way to be truly satisfied is to do what you believe is great work."
  • Numerous quotes by Steve Jobs reference innovation, including, "Innovation distinguishes between a leader and a follower."
  • Courage's clothing bears resemblance to the typical dress of Jobs and his accessories to Apple products.
  • Courage's music device seems to be fashioned after an Apple iPod.
  • Jobs' trademark clothing included a black turtleneck and white New Balance sneakers, while Courage begins wearing a black sweater at Rank 2 and white sneakers at Rank 4.
  • At Rank 2, Courage's iPod has regular wired earbuds. However, at Rank 4 Courage has wireless earbuds, possibly modeled on Apple AirPods.
  • Courage also gains wire-frame glasses at Rank 4 and a distinctly Steve Jobs-like smirk.
  • Courage's description could be a reference to Jobs being one of the youngest people to make the list of the United States' wealthiest citizens, with a worth of over $100 million by the age of 25.
