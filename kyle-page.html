The facts

Kyle's dialog appears to be a nod to role-playing games.

  • His line, "Enchantment?", might be taken from a line spoken by Sandal Feddic in the RPG Dragon Age: Origins: "Enchantment? Enchantment!"
  • His line about giving a weapon +1 refers to a feature in old-school RPGs, such as Dungeon & Dragons, in which some equipment can be enhanced "+1" or "+2", etc., by enchantment.
  • According to the third mini-story in the Anniversary 2018 Storyline, Kyle can use fire and is "also a master of the water element".
  • He also uses his water magic in the Summer 2020 Quest, when trying to one-up Francis
  • The event stories often portray Kyle as someone who loves himself and thinks he is better than others.
  • Kyle is a participant in Ember, Catania's version of the dating app Tinder that has appeared in the game during Valentine Events since 2021; his profile reads as follows: The awesomest cat you will ever meet. You need to be at least an 8 to swipe right. Look at the pics, that's all you need.
  • Kyle's Halloween Hero Outfit, known in the wiki as "Sparkling Vampire Kyle", sparkles, likely a reference to the "sparkly" vampires of the Twilight franchise.
  • Many fans ship Kyle and Francis, and fanart of the two was posted on the Castle Cats Facebook page on Father's Day 2021, suggesting the occasion be celebrated by "remembering that all families are different!"
  • Although a Castle Cats developaw explicitly stated in a comment on the post that the Francis-Kyle ship is not canon, the Alchemy cat Franklyn appears to be a "merging" of the two.
  • In the Call of Heroes 2022 Campaign, one quest, titled after the pair's ship name, "Frankyle", depicts Kyle with an unexplained bald spot; coincidentally, or perhaps not, tufts of fur from both members of a pair are required to make a shipkit, as described when Purrcilla took one from Francis in the Valentine 2017 Quest.
  • According to the 2022 Ask-The-Devs-Anything, Kyle has an older sister and a younger brother.
  • When they were younger, Kyle's older sister tried to offer him up for adoption to strangers on the street because they were fighting so much.
