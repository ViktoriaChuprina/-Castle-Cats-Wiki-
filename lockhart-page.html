Lockhart
Lockhart is a Mythic Valentine Purchase Hero who is not currently available for recruitment.
Lockhart is available annually in the Valentine Events, offered originally in 2024 for $4.99 USD and from 2025 onward for 300 gems and as a possible reward in the Event Box.
Lockhart will also be eligible to become a Timewalker for 300 gems but has not appeared as one as of yet.
