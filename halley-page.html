The facts

Halley was one of five winners of the December 2017–January 2018 Design a Castle Cat contest[1], designed by @wyveram.

  • Halley's name might come from the Halley's Comet Opal, the world's largest recorded uncut black opal.
  • Halley's dialog includes a couple of puns on rocks, but the material in which she is encapsulated, opal, is actually a mineraloid rather than a type of rock, sedimentary or otherwise.
