The facts

Pink's appearance and name might have been inspired by the pop singer P!nk.

  • Pink's pet rock Pebble, noted in her Hero Book entry, could be a nod to Maud Pie, sister of Pinkie Pie, of My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic, who is a rock enthusiast and has a pet rock named Boulder.
  • Pink's hairstyle and its color might be inspired by those of Trish Una from the Japanese manga JoJo's Bizarre Adventure.
  • Pink might be an American Wirehair, a breed known for its springy, wiry coat and sturdy build.
