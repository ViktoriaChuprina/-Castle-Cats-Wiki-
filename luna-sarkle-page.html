The facts

Luna Sparkle is most likely based on the characters Princess Luna and Twilight Sparkle from the animated television series My Little Pony: Friendship Is Magic.

  • One of her lines is "Friendship is magic."
  • Luna Sparkle says she's an alicorn, a pony with both Pegasus wings and a unicorn horn, as are Princess Luna and later Twilight Sparkle.
  • The Anniversary 2018 Event Story reveals that Luna Sparkle can walk in dreams like Princess Luna.
  • Celestia, Cherry, June, Minty Heart and Starsky are other Castle Cats heroes who are probably derived from particular characters in the My Little Pony franchise.
  • Her line about being "the fluffiest of them all" might be a play on the desire of the Queen from the fairy tale "Snow White" to be "the fairest of them all".
  • Alternatively it could be Luna trying to one-up a unicorn plushie in the movie Despicable Me, about which the character Agnes shouts, "It's so fluffy!"
  • Luna inadvertently takes a magical object belonging to Harbour, hindering his ability to avert disaster in the Summer 2019 Quest.
