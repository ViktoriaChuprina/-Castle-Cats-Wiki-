The facts

Curio is the main character in PocApp Studios' second/previous game, Sling A Kitty

  • Her description about chasing butterflies is a reference to Sling a Kitty, as is the butterfly net held by her tail.
  • There is a debate within the fandom as to whether Curio is female or male due to a typo in the storefront of Sling a Kitty.
  • Curio's fur is also available as a customization in PocApp Studios' first game, Carpet Kitty.
  • "Curio" means something unusual or bizarre, or an unusual or bizarre person; it could also be a shortening of "curious" or "curiosity," perhaps in reference to the proverb, "Curiosity killed the cat."
  • Conan is Curio's older brother, and Kitty is her older sister.
  • Curio and Bob are best friends and often appear together in event stories.
