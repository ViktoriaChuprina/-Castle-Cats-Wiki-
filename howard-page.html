The facts

Howard is a confirmed reference to Cthulhu, a fictional being from the works of Howard P. Lovecraft

  • This is indicated by the squidlike features he gains at Rank 2, as well as his original name, Cathulhu, before it was changed in an update.
  • His current name is the same as the author's, and he has the Crafty skill, as in Lovecraft.
  • His line, "Always fear the unknown," looks like a reference to a quote by Lovecraft: "The oldest and strongest emotion of mankind is fear, and the oldest and strongest kind of fear is fear of the unknown."
  • It was theorized by some players that Howard might be Francis' father due to their almost identical body shapes and connections to mystical power; but Howard has since been redesigned, and his Rank 1 image no longer bears a strong resemblance to Francis.
  • Howard is a fan-inspired cat, suggested by wilcox1232 on the Castle Cats subreddit.
