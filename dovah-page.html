The facts

Dovah is most likely a reference to the game The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim, an open-world fantasy RPG

  • Dovah is named after the word for dragon in the RPG's in-game dragon language.
  • Dovah's Rank 2 outfit resembles that of a Skyrim Hold Guard, and at Rank 4 he dons a helmet inspired by the game's iconic Iron Helmet.
  • At Rank 4 Dovah sports an arrow that sticks out of his hind leg, referencing a phrase commonly spoken by Hold Guards: "I used to be an adventurer like you, but then I took an arrow to the knee."
  • The phrase later became a jokey internet meme.
  • Dovah's final Hero Book description is a reference to a popular albeit incorrect theory that taking an arrow in the knee is a euphemism for getting married.
  • One of Dovah's lines, "*ahem* Stop right there, criminal!", echoes a common line spoken by guards in Skyrim's predecessor, Oblivion, "Stop right there, criminal scum!", which also quickly became popular on the internet as a humorous meme.
