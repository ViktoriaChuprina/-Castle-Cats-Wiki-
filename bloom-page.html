The facts

Bloom appears to be based on the character Legolas Greenleaf from The Lord of the Rings, a high-fantasy novel by J.R.R. Tolkien.

  • Bloom's name comes from Orlando Bloom, the actor who plays Legolas in The Lord of the Rings movies.
  • His line, "Two arrows at once? No problem," reflects Legolas' ability to shoot two arrows at once.
  • His line, "They're taking the kittens to Embergard!", is a play on Legolas' line, "They're taking the Hobbits to Isengard!"
