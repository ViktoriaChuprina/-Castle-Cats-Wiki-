The facts

Alfa was modeled and named after a real-life cat belonging to PocApp Studios developaw Nilla.

  • The real-life Alfa has since passed on.
  • Alfa's line about "third breakfast" is probably a nod to a comment in the film The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring in which a pair of hobbits, who, like Alfa, are always hungry, are dismayed about not getting their "second breakfast".
