The facts

Dory was modeled after a real-life cat named Doris belonging to a relative of a PocApp Studios developaw.

  • Dory's appearance was updated in version 2.5.1 to reflect her real-life counterpart having grown from kitten to adult.
  • Dory is a combat sports enthusiast, as evidenced in part by much of her dialog.
  • Dory loves Muay Thai, commonly known as Thai boxing.
  • Her line, "Float like a butterfly, sting like a bee...", is a quote by famous boxer Muhammad Ali.
  • Dory is a Ragdoll, a breed characterized by a silky coat with point coloration and blue eyes.
