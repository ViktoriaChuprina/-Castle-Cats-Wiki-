The facts

Loka was formerly named Loki and was a premium Charity Event cat until October, 2020, when in version 2.14 her name was changed and she became a Mythic Box Hero.

  • Loki was offered for sale only once, for $2.99, during the Charity Event 2017.
  • Like all premium Charity cats, Loki was a Celebrity Hero, a rendition and namesake of a real-life celebrity cat, available in the game by official arrangement with the cat's real-life owner.
  • Loki's Hero Book description was the same as Loka's is now, except it also included a link to her real-life counterpart's Instagram page.
  • Loki's lines were the same as those now said by Loka, except for the name difference.
  • The cat also underwent a significant redesign as part of the changeover; her former appearance as Loki is pictured below.
