Event Box

This box is only available during the Event and is located in the Event Store. At first you can open it 1 time for free. Subsequent times require some costs: every 2 days the box can be opened for 50,000 gold, and the rest of the time - for 60 diamonds. In the Event Box you can get various resources: Rare Lights, Event Materials, Diamonds, Costumes, Reputation Points , as well as Premium Event Heroes (only those that were entered earlier), which appear in the box with a 2% chance. The box can always be reopened or its contents updated, but in both cases this requires diamonds (except for the 1st update, it occurs for viewing an advertisement).

Cats from Events are only available during this event, and therefore only then can they be brought out.

Donate

It is rather inappropriate to deny the existence of donation in the game. Some packages in the Exclusive Store include common, rare, mythical cats, and Celebrity Heroes.

Also in the Event Store you can often buy new (usually recently introduced into the game) Premium Event Heroes for real money. When purchasing them in the game, the drop of Event Materials increases several times (maximum 3) and most of the advertising is removed.

Heroes for reputation

There are currently 3 such heroes in the game. They can be obtained from level 50 of the Reputation of a specific guild ( Mustache, Mighty Lions, Zenpaws ). To develop relations with the guild, you must contribute Reputation Points .

Daily Rewards

Log into the game every day and on a certain day you may be given a cat.

Buy with Diamonds

You can unlock any cat (except mythical, legendary, alchemical and event cats) in the Book of Heroes for Diamonds . A regular cat can be bought for 30 diamonds, a rare one for 90. Calendar heroes can be bought in the book only if you have already received them in daily rewards and for some reason were released, or you did not receive them due to an in-game error. The mythical cat from new versions can only be purchased in the Time Machine , as a Traveler, and in a box. Sometimes you can also purchase Event Heroes in the Time Machine . The cost of purchasing them depends on the rarity of the hero (regular - 100 diamonds, rare - 200 diamonds, mythical - 300 diamonds).

Withdraw in Alchemy

In Alchemy you can not breed all cats, but only a few. These cats cannot fall out of boxes and cannot be purchased with diamonds. To hatch them, you will need some ingredients (a certain amount of materials or cats falling out of boxes).

During Alchemy Events , you can breed special cats. There are 2 ways to do this:

1.For Event Materials
2.From other cats of this Event , namely from those that are created in Alchemy for Event Materials.

Cats from Events are only available during this event, and therefore only then can they be brought out.
