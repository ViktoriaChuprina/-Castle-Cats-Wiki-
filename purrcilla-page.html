The facts

Purrcilla is probably the name Priscilla altered to make a cat pun, of which the Castle Cats developaws are exceedingly fond.

  • Purrcilla could be regarded as the public face of Castle Cats
  • Her image is used as the icon for the app and as the profile picture for the game's social media accounts.
  • When launching the game for the first time, Purrcilla is the first cat who appears and guides the player through the tutorial
  • During the tutorial when she first meets Francis, Purrcilla gasps and later remarks, "That mage... is the most beautiful cat I have ever seen!", thus beginning a continuing storyline of unrequited love.
  • In the Valentine 2017 Quest, Purrcilla uses a potion to create her and Francis' child, Purrcis, without Francis' knowledge or consent.
  • The pair finally go out on a date in the Valentine 2021 Quest, and Purrcilla discovers to her dismay that Francis is rather boorish and boring.
  • In the Call of Heroes 2021 Quest Purrcilla, still stewing over Francis, adopts the supervillain persona of Lady Paradox and uses dark magic to open a portal to the past so she can prevent herself from falling in love with him in the first place.
  • A Hero Outfit that gives Purrcilla her Lady Paradox appearance is available for purchase during Call of Heroes Events.
