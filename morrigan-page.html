The facts

Morrigan's description is likely a reference to a Swedish legend about a witch gathering on the island of Blockula. In Swedish, Blockula or "Blåkulla" translates to "Blue Hill".

  • Morrigan might be a reference to the Morrígan of Irish mythology.
  • She could also be a reference to the character Morrigan from the Dragon Age series of RPGs.
  • Morrigan's quote about a delivery service is probably a reference to the Japanese animated film Kiki's Delivery Service.
  • Morrigan was confirmed by the developaws as lesbian on International Lesbian Day, 2022.
  • Morrigan is a participant in Ember, Catania's version of the dating app Tinder that has appeared in the game during Valentine Events since 2021; her profile reads as follows: Read this then message me how you feel: "Love is a lie, I feel nothing. Love is a lie, I feel nothing." (Yes, this is a curse.)
