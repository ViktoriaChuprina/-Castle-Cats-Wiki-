The facts

Catniss and Edward officially became a couple in the Valentine 2024 Quest.

  • Catniss, Conan and Francis are the main heroes of the game, the first to join the guild (after the tutorial hostess), the first three cats in the Hero Book, and the lead characters in the Main Story Quest.
  • Catniss' locked silhouette is actually visible when purchasing her through the Hero Book for 30 gems
  • Catniss is almost certainly based on the character Katniss Everdeen from The Hunger Games trilogy and films.
  • One of her lines is, "Perfect day for some hungry games."
  • According to a photo on the official Castle Cats Facebook account, Catniss' last name is Everclean.
  • Like Katniss, Catniss is an archer.
  • Catniss' willingness to volunteer, as her Hero Book description mentions, might refer to Katniss volunteering to take the place of her sister in the Hunger Games.
  • Katniss and Catniss both have sisters named after flowers.
