The facts

Bob's name and his Crafty skill might be a reference to the children's show Bob the Builder.

  • Bob really, really, REALLY wants to fly.
  • The fandom is currently on a quest to make this happen, using tags such as #whataboutbob, #givebobwings, and, of course, #letbobfly.
  • Bob does fly, sort of, when equipped with his "Christmas Sled Bob" Hero Outfit.
  • Bob's Hero Book descriptions are lyrics from Randy Newman's song, "I Will Go Sailing No More," from the Toy Story soundtrack, which plays while Buzz Lightyear learns that he is a toy and cannot fly.
  • Bob's Sneaky skill might be a reference to the Metal Gear Solid character Solid Snake, considering he is in a box at the time he gets it.
  • Bob and Curio are best friends, and they often appear together in event stories.
