The facts

Molotov is named after the Molotov cocktail, a homemade bottle bomb created by Finns during the Winter War in 1939-1940.

  • His Hero Book entry is a clear reference to this incendiary device.
  • He is a self-proclaimed "bomber".
  • His line, "Explosions excite me!", could refer to the character Megumin from the KonoSuba: God’s Blessing on this Wonderful World! series.
  • Molotov is afraid of heights.
  • Ever since the Spring Festival 2018 Quest, Molotov has been Bob's and Curio's Spring Festival guardian, a role for which he dresses as the Easter Bunny.
  • According to Benedict in the Valentine 2020 Quest, Molotov’s real name is Aleksey.
  • Molotov and Pipsqueak have a kitten named Ash, who was added to the game as an Alchemy cat in the Valentine Event 2020.
