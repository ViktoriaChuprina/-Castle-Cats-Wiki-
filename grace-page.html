Grace
Grace is a Mythic Spring Festival Purchase Hero who is not currently available for recruitment.
Grace is available annually in the Spring Festival Events, offered originally in 2024 for $4.99 USD and from 2025 onward for 300 gems and as a possible reward in the Event Box.
Grace is also eligible to become a Timewalker for 300 gems but has not appeared as one as of yet.

The facts

Grace may be a reference to equestrianism

  • Her formal outfit and black boots resemble those worn by riders in dressage competitions.
  • The horseshoe on her bag and mane-like hair and tail are other references to horses.
