The facts

Azure's name and appearance, as well as his status as a guardian of an important object, appear to reference the Azure Ring Guardian, an elite Dragonkin mob in the MMO World of Warcraft.

  • Some of the fandom's candidates for who committed the theft of Azure's artifact include Francis, Dovah, Nyadra, Nolan and The Evil Pugomancer, who drops a magical gem at the start of the Main Story Quest. It should be noted that none of them would have done it on their own, as Azure's description accuses "some adventurers" (plural), and not "an adventurer" (singular).
