The facts

Lois might be named after Lois Pryce, a British adventurer, travel author, journalist and broadcaster.

  • Lois might be a lynx, as she was once mistakenly called "Lynx" in an in-game daily rewards window.
  • Upon release, Lois could be acquired in the Rare cat box, an unintentional issue[1] that has since been patched.
  • Beans' Purrismas outfit was originally a cat, named Catpuccino, entered in the 5th Design a Castle Cats Contest by maddeedraws, but the developaws decided to adapt it as a Hero Outfit for Beans, purchasable for gems using the Armory feature in the Fortress.
