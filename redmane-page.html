The facts

Redmane might be based on the character Ajani Goldmane from the collectible trading card game Magic: The Gathering.

  • They have similar names.
  • Both characters have a scar over one eye, wear teal clothing with gold epaulets or shoulder armor, and wield a double-ended axe.
  • Redmane "travels between worlds", according to his Hero Book description, and claims to be a "Realmjumper", which might parallel Goldmane's status as a Planeswalker who can move between universes.
  • His lines about his "spark" and being "tapped out" also appear to be references to concepts in the card game.
  • Lotus is another Castle Cats hero with an apparent reference to the MTG franchise
  • Redmane is one of several Castle Cats heroes who is missing an eye; the others are Agatha, Ratchet, Turrim, Wetbeard and Yule.
