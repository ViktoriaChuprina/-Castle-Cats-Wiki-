The facts

Athea was modeled after a real-life cat named Nala belonging to PocApp Studios developaw Ellinor.

  • The in-game character's name was changed to avoid confusion with the hero already named Nala.
  • The drink Athea is holding features a circular logo and is a confirmed reference to the Starbucks chain of coffee houses.
  • Athea's lines seem to reference the 2004 movie Mean Girls and the character Gretchen Wieners, played by Lacey Chabert, who also said, "That is so fetch!"
  • Upon release, Athea's name, quotes and Hero Book entry were missing; the glitch was fixed in a quick update.
  • Athea has a younger brother named Tyrus, who can also be obtained in the Rare Hero box.
