The facts

Coco was probably inspired by two figures associated with the House of Chanel.

  • Coco might have been named after Coco Chanel, founder of the fashion design company.
  • Coco's white ponytail and dark glasses were signature features of Chanel's creative director and well-known designer Karl Lagerfeld, who passed away shortly before Coco's introduction.
  • Coco is a Birman who looks very much like Choupette, Lagerfeld's cat of the same breed.
