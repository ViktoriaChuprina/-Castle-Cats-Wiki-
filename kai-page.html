The facts

Kai might be based on a character with the same name from the action-adventure game Ōkami

  • They share a somewhat similar color scheme.
  • The Ōkami character is able to shapeshift, as Kai was revealed to be able to do in the Purrismas 2017 Quest.
  • The line, "It's warpaint, not makeup," mirrors a line said by the character Sokka in Avatar: The Last Airbender.
  • She is also one of the spirit pets that can be used with Charlie.
  • Kai's shapeshifting abilities are further explored in the Halloween 2020 Quest, in which she reveals a dark form in the course of contending with the manticore Xerxes.
  • When she appears in event stories, Kai frequently addresses her companions as "friend".
  • Kai is a participant in Ember, Catania's version of the dating app Tinder that has appeared in the game during Valentine Events since 2021; her profile reads as follows: If you like the strange magic of nature and slightly forbidden, we may surely become friends. I live in a hut. Tea-time is a must every single day.
