The facts

Cleo was one of five winners of the December 2017–January 2018 Design a Castle Cat contest, designed by Geovanna Ollivierre-w.

  • Cleo's name and appearance suggest she is a reference to the pharaohs and culture of ancient Egypt.
  • Cleo is a shortening of Cleopatra, a name shared by several queens of the Ptolemaic dynasty of Egypt.
  • Her Hero Book description mentions that Cleo spreads beauty, something for which the most famous Cleopatra is renowned.
  • She wears a headcloth known as a nemes and worn by Egyptian pharaohs.
  • In her tail she holds an ankh, the Egyptian symbol of life.
  • Her designer says Cleo is a Siamese Sphynx cat, a breed named after the Great Sphinx of Giza, with pointed coloration
  • Cleo was originally named Isis, after the Egyptian goddess, but her name was changed to avoid negative modern connotations.
