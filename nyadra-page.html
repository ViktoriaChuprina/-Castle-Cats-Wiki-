The facts

Nyadra might be a reference to the character Smaug from J.R.R. Tolkien's novel The Hobbit and its film adaptations.

  • Nyadra's Hero Book entry describes her as a hoarder of gold and gems, as is Smaug.
  • Her line, "I am fire! I am ...", echoes one spoken by Smaug in one of the live-action movies: "I am fire! I am Death!"
  • Her line, "By fire be purged!", is possibly taken from Ragnaros, a boss character who says the same thing in the MMORPG World of Warcraft.
  • Her name could be a combination of dragon with naga, a mythical dragon-like creature.
  • Nyadra's name could also be based on the Japanese word for meow, nya.
  • In the Summer 2019 Event Story, Nyadra and Nessie are revealed to be estranged from a former friendship after they had a big fight.
  • Nyadra is a participant in Ember, Catania's version of the dating app Tinder that has appeared in the game during Valentine Events since 2021; her profile reads as follows: I dislike most people.
