The facts

Nessie is probably named after the Loch Ness Monster, a cryptozoological entity also known as Nessie.

  • Her description suggests she was also inspired by the mythical Sirens, who would sing to passing sailors and induce slumber, madness, shipwreck or death.
  • Her line, "La la la la ~", would probably then be her siren song.
  • She was originally called a catfish—now contradicted by her line, "I'm a fishcat!"—as a pun on actual catfish.
  • Her line, "Mrglglglg!", is likely a reference to Murlocs from the MMO World of Warcraft.
  • Nessie is the sister of Harbour, her former singing partner until she left to become a hero
  • Harbour resented his sister for leaving, but they reconcile in the Summer 2019 Quest.
  • Nessie and Harbour have nicknames for each other: Seesee for the former and Beebee for the latter.
  • In the same story, Nessie and Nyadra are revealed to be estranged from a former friendship after they had a big fight.
  • Nessie was the first ever calendar cat, available at the end of the original 5-day calendar.
