The facts

Conan can be "promoted" by crafting a special outfit offered on a rotating basis as one of the Monthly Challenges. The outfit is strictly cosmetic and has no special perks or hidden features.

  • Juliet is partly based on the female lead in the Shakespearean play Romeo and Juliet
  • One of Juliet's lines is derived from one of the most famous in the play: "Wherefore art thou Romeo?"
  • As a Dynamic Duo cat, Juliet is paired with Romeo, Castle Cats' counterpart to the male lead in the play.
  • Romeo and Juliet are usually together when they appear in event stories and were the main characters in the Valentine 2018 Quest.
  • The appearance of Charmer in the same quest caused some jealousy between the couple and hinted at their bisexual nature, which was later confirmed by the developaws.
  • While not explicitly acknowledged in-game, it is believed that Julio is the couple's son, as his name is a blending of theirs
  • When addressing Conan in event stories, Juliet often uses the nickname "Con-Con", which might be connected to Juliet's being a thief and con artist, someone who steals through trickery and fraud.
  • Juliet is a participant in Ember, Catania's version of the dating app Tinder that has appeared in the game during Valentine Events since 2021; her profile reads as follows: Competing with my beloved. Place your paw on that like button and I'll message you something nice (maybe).
