The facts

Dango is named after a Japanese sweet called Dango.

  • As a Dynamic Duo cat, he is paired with Mochi, another hero named for a Japanese food.
  • His line, "Special move: RASENYAN!", is possibly a reference to the technique Rasengan from the Naruto manga franchise.
  • His line, "Orewa shinobi, da yo," translates to "I am a ninja."
  • Dango is one of several Castle Cats heroes with heterochromatic eyes; the others include Charlie, Dexter, Ferdinand, Franklyn and Sandra.
